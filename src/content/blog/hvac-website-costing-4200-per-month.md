$4,200 per month.

That's the average revenue a typical HVAC contractor loses because of their website. Not because they don't have one — because the one they have isn't working.

Here's the breakdown.

## The math

### Slow mobile site: -$1,800/mo

Your site takes [18 seconds to load on a phone](/blog/18-seconds-your-customer-left-after-3/). The industry standard is under 3.

About 3 visitors per day leave before it loads. At a 15% booking rate and $400 average job, that's roughly $1,800/month you never see.

### No click-to-call: -$1,200/mo

60% of HVAC searches are on mobile. People want to tap and call. If your phone number is just text in the footer, about 2 potential callers per day give up and try someone else.

That's another $1,200 gone.

### No after-hours form: -$800/mo

AC dies at midnight. Pipe bursts on Sunday. 46% of HVAC emergencies happen after hours.

If there's no way to reach you — no form, no chat, no answering service — that's roughly 1 emergency lead per week going to a competitor. At premium emergency rates, that's $800/month minimum.

### "Not Secure" warning: -$400/mo

No SSL certificate means Chrome shows a "Not Secure" warning to every visitor. About 15% bounce immediately just from seeing that.

Conservative cost: $400/month.

## Total: $4,200/month. $50,000/year.

And that's the conservative estimate. Contractors with higher traffic or in competitive markets lose more.

## Why this keeps happening

Most HVAC business owners don't look at their own website on a phone. They built it years ago on a desktop, approved it on a desktop, and haven't thought about it since.

Meanwhile, their customers are Googling "AC repair near me" on an iPhone at 2pm when the house hits 90 degrees. And the website that loads in 18 seconds is not the one they're calling.

## The fix isn't expensive

Every problem in that $4,200 breakdown can be fixed for less than one month's lost revenue:

- **Speed optimization:** $300-800 (one-time)
- **Click-to-call setup:** $100-200
- **After-hours form:** $200-400
- **SSL certificate:** Free to $50/year

Total fix cost: roughly $600-$1,400. One-time.

Monthly recovery: $4,200+. Every month. Forever.

That's not marketing. That's math.
